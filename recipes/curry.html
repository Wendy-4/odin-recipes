Chicken Curry

Description

This quick and tasty chicken curry goes well with either a naan bread or rice.

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken, diced
  • 1 onion
  • Curry sauce of choice
  • Coriander (to garnish)
  • Either naan bread or rice as accompaniment

Steps

  1. If having rice, boil a pan of water and cook the rice for 20 mins.
  2. Chop the onion and fry off for a couple of minutes.
  3. Add the diced chicken to the onions and fry until browned.
  4. Add the sauce and simmer for 10 mins
  5. If having naan bread, place under the grill or in an air fryer for a couple of minutes either side.
  6. Place onto your plate and serve. Enjoy!
