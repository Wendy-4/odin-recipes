Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

This is a quick and tasty spaghetti dish made with beef on top of a bed of spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince
  • Can of chopped tomatoes
  • Tomato puree
  • 200g dried spaghetti
  • Parmesan cheese (to garnish)

Steps

  1. Boil a pan of water for the spaghetti. Add a small amount of oil to the water to help the spaghetti to not stick.
  2. Heat a small amount of oil in a pan and add the beef mince.
  3. When the beef is cooked through, add the chopped tomatoes and tomato puree and stir.
  4. Turn the heat down to simmer and cook for 10 mins.
  5. When the spaghetti has cooked, place in a dish and add the beef on top.
  6. Grate some parmesan cheese on top and enjoy!
